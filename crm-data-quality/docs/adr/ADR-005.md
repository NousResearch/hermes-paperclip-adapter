# ADR-005: Canonical Data Contract Versioning

## Status
Accepted

## Context
Entity attributes and scoring requirements will evolve. Without explicit contract versioning, producers and consumers can drift and break integrations.

## Decision
Version canonical contracts semantically (`vMAJOR.MINOR`) and require explicit compatibility notes for each schema change.

## Consequences
- Positive: Controlled evolution with predictable upgrade paths.
- Positive: Easier rollback during incident response.
- Negative: Requires governance overhead for change reviews.
- Negative: Temporary dual-version support may be needed.

## Alternatives Considered
- Unversioned schemas with latest-only behavior: rejected due to hidden breaking changes.
- Timestamp-based ad hoc versions: rejected due to weak compatibility semantics.
