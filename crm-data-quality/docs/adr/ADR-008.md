# ADR-008: Data Privacy and Field Minimization

## Status
Accepted

## Context
CRM entities may include personal and sensitive fields not needed for quality scoring. Broad extraction increases compliance and exposure risk.

## Decision
Apply field minimization: extract only approved attributes required by current rules and mask sensitive values in logs and diagnostics.

## Consequences
- Positive: Lower privacy risk and smaller data footprint.
- Positive: Reduced accidental disclosure in telemetry.
- Negative: Rule authors must request field additions through governance.
- Negative: Some troubleshooting requires privileged replay paths.

## Alternatives Considered
- Extract full entity payloads then filter downstream: rejected due to unnecessary data exposure.
- No logging restrictions: rejected due to compliance and security concerns.
